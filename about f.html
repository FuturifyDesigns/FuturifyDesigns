01 — My Story

My name is Leon Maunge, and I'm a 4th year Computer Science student at the University of Botswana. Originally from Zambia, I now call Botswana home, where I've been pursuing my passion for technology and digital innovation.

My journey into web design began during my Web Development and Technology course. What started as academic learning quickly transformed into a burning passion. I was captivated by the power of design — how colors, layouts, and interactions could tell stories and create unforgettable user experiences.

Since that pivotal moment, I've been on a relentless quest to master the art of creating dynamic and interactive websites. Every project is an opportunity to push the boundaries of what's possible on the web.

I specialize in creating stunning informational websites that captivate and engage — from personal portfolios to business websites, restaurant sites to creative agency showcases.

I am a full stack developer, capable of building complete end-to-end solutions with database integration and server-side functionality. My code is clean, well-documented, and ready for any backend integration.

What drives me is the belief that great design can change the world. A well-crafted website isn't just beautiful — it's functional, accessible, and built with purpose.
