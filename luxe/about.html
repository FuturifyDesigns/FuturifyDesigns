Our Commitment

A Legacy of Craftsmanship

Since 1985

Founded in a small workshop in Florence, LUXE began with a simple mission: to create furniture that stands the test of time. Our founder, Giovanni Rossi, believed that true luxury lies in the details - the hand-carved embellishments, the carefully selected materials, and the passion poured into every piece.

Today, we continue this tradition with a team of master craftsmen who combine time-honored techniques with contemporary design. Each piece in our collection tells a story of dedication, from the initial sketch to the final polish.

Our Journey

Milestones in Excellence

From humble beginnings to international recognition, our journey is marked by dedication to craftsmanship and innovation.

1985

Founding of LUXE

Giovanni Rossi establishes his workshop in Florence, specializing in handcrafted wooden furniture with renaissance influences.

1992

First International Exhibition

Our pieces debut at the Milan Furniture Fair, earning critical acclaim and our first international clients.

2001

Expansion to New York

Opening of our first showroom outside Italy, marking the beginning of our global presence.

2010

Sustainable Practices

Full transition to ethically sourced materials and implementation of zero-waste production processes.

2020

Third Generation

Isabella Rossi joins the company, bringing fresh perspectives while preserving traditional techniques.

The Art of Furniture Making

Where Tradition Meets Innovation

At LUXE, we believe furniture should be more than functional—it should be a work of art. Our process begins with sustainably sourced materials from trusted suppliers around the world. From there, our artisans employ techniques passed down through generations.

Material Selection

Only the finest hardwoods, metals, and fabrics are selected for their quality and durability. Each material is carefully inspected for grain, color, and structural integrity.

Handcrafted Details

Each piece is shaped and assembled by hand, ensuring perfect joints and seamless finishes. Our artisans spend up to 200 hours on a single piece.

Finishing Touches

Our proprietary finishing techniques enhance natural beauty while protecting your furniture for generations. Each finish is hand-applied and polished to perfection.

Sustainable Luxury

Responsible Craftsmanship

At LUXE, we believe true luxury must be sustainable. That's why we source materials responsibly, minimize waste in our production process, and create pieces designed to last for generations.

We partner with organizations that plant trees for every piece sold and use only non-toxic finishes that are safe for your family and the environment. Our workshop runs on 100% renewable energy, and we've reduced our carbon footprint by 65% since 2010.
