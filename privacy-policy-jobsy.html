Privacy policy

Jobsy · your data is safe with us

Effective date: 5 May 2026 · Applies to the Jobsy mobile app (package com.futurify.jobsy)

This policy explains how Jobsy handles personal data when you use the app—the same summaries you’ll find under Privacy & Security in Jobsy settings, expanded slightly so Google Play visitors have a readable, complete page.

Who this covers

Jobsy serves people looking for shifts or flexible work (workers) and people posting jobs (employers). Sections below call out differences where it matters.

How we protect your data

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data.
  • Secure storage with hardened infrastructure.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • Processes aligned with GDPR and POPIA where those laws apply.

What we collect

Depending on how you use Jobsy:

  • Profile & account: name, phone number, and location-related details you choose to provide.
  • If you hire: job postings you create and applications you receive through the platform.
  • If you work: job applications you send and relevant work history in the product.
  • Payments: transaction or billing history maintained in encrypted form as needed for the service.
  • Product analytics: usage signals in aggregated or anonymised form so we can keep the app reliable.

How we use information

We use collected data to operate Jobsy: matching workers with employers, processing applications and listings, honouring encrypted payment records where they apply to the service, meeting legal retention rules when required, and keeping the platform stable.

Sharing with others

We only share personal data where it supports the Jobsy platform: for instance with vendors that host databases or send transactional messages under contract, where the law requires disclosure, or with your consent. We remain responsible for safeguarding data aligned with this policy.

Your rights

  • Access the personal data Jobsy holds about you.
  • Correct or refresh information tied to your account.
  • Request deletion of data we no longer need to operate the service or retain for law.
  • Export your data in a standard, portable format upon request.

Security measures

  • Optional two-factor authentication for accounts that support it.
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity tied to protecting accounts.
  • Regular security audits and reviews.
  • Automatic session timeouts to reduce unauthorised access risk.
Questions
For privacy enquiries, email futurifydesigns@gmail.com.

Children

Jobsy is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal data without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a child supplied information in error, contact us—we will promptly review removal options.

Changes

If we materially change practices, we will update this page and revise the effective date above. Continuing to use Jobsy after publication means acceptance of reasonable updates aligned with expanded features or tighter protection.
